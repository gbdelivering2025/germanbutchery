German Butchery – WhatsApp Order

This demo shows how a customer can select flexible quantities (g/kg/pc/pkt) and send an order via WhatsApp to the shop owner. This uses WhatsApp Click-to-Chat and opens WhatsApp with a prefilled order message.

Bratwurst (1,200 RWF per pc)
Ground Beef (3,000 RWF per kg)
